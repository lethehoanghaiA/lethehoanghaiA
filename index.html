About me

I graduated major Economic Investment at National Economics University in VietNam. Fours year working for three companies made me realise the success of modern businesses mostly depends on the Technology advantage they have to their competitors. Though every company claimed they wanted a digital transformation, they didn't have enough insights and knowledge to actualize that wish. The shortest of Tech people in the corporation make new project so hard to launch whenever new Technology involved. Working in R&D, I was given all the jobs relating research new software Technologies. That's why I started to learn programming on my own.
After three months struggling with self-taught process, I wrote my first Java "Battle Ship game". That time made me realize I'd already had a great passion for programming. Moreover, I thought I could do it quite well and enjoy the process. It would be awesome if I could make a living as a Software developer. So I decided to quit my stable job and spend all of my time learning I.T. Then, I enrolled a coding Bootcamp at Techmaster coding center which requires high intensive practice of 8 hours a day. At home, I spent hours watching programming tutorials and reading technical programming book. Now, my career goal is becoming a professional Software Engineer at a big Tech company, where I can grow, learn and assert all my potentials. I am the type of person who is determined, self-motivated to achieve my goals. If your company is a place where ambitious people gather to make fantastic software products, I'm glad to have a chance for further discussion.
