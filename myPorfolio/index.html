About me

I graduated major Economic Investment at National Economics University in VietNam. Fours year working for companies made me realised the huge differences Technologies could make between businesses success and failure. Though every company claims they want a digital transformation, most don't have enough insights and knowledge to actualize that wish. The shortest of Tech experts in my company made me realize I have to explore this domain. So I spent my time out of work to learn coding on my own.
After three months struggling with self-taught process, I wrote my first Java "Battle Ship game". That time made me realized I've already had a great passion for programming. Moreover, I thought I could do it quite well and enjoy the process. I want to be an expert in this. So I decided to play all-in, I quit my stable job and spend all of my time learning I.T. I enrolled a coding Bootcamp at Techmaster coding center which requires high intensive practice of 8 hours a day. Beside, I also spent hours watching programming tutorials and reading technical programming book. My goal is to become a professional Software Engineer at a big Tech company, where I can grow, learn and assert all my potentials. I am the type of person who is determined, self-motivated to achieve my goals. Looking forward to seeing you and your company!
